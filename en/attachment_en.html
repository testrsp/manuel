Spread Link

Click here/Unsubscribe

  1. Input text
  2. Highlight the words: Click here/Unsubscribe
  3. Click "Spread Link"
  4. Choose "Click here"/"Unsubscribe"
  5. Click "Finish"
    • Result: Click here
    • Result: Unsubscribe

Add PDF Attachment

  • File size smaller than 1M is supported.
  1. Click "My Account" and then click "Settings"
    1. Method 1
    2. Method 2
  2. Tick the Enable Attachments & Save
  3. Back to create a new campaign and save first
  4. Find the “Attachment” button in the bottom of the Email Editing Interface.
  5. “Attachment” window will be poped up. Click “Add files” and choose the file from your PC.

  6. You can “Preview” or “Delete” the files you uploaded.
  7. You will find the (number) of Attachments uploaded.
