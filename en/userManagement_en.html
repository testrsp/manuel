  2. Grant right to another account and edit right

    To add another existing account

    1. 點擊“用戶管理”
    2. 輸入另一已註冊帳戶的登入電郵
    3. Click “User Management”

    To add a account not yet registered

    1. Type a Login Email Address
    2. Choose the role then click “Add”
    3. Warning “Email not found” will be shown
    4. Set the “Password” for the account
    5. Click “Add” again
      Role Default right granted
      Basic User View all email campaigns
      Email Marketer View all email campaigns, view email addresses, view usage report, upload emails, send campaign, download email address and export campaign report
      Administrators View all email campaigns, view email addresses, view usage report and send campaign
    6. Edit the right of individual account by clicking “edit”
    7. Tick to rights, and click “update”
      Right Details
      View All Campaigns To view all campaigns
      View Email addresses To view and export contact lists
      View Usage Report To read usage reports
      Upload Emails To upload contact lists
      Send Campaign To send campaign
      Approve Campaign To approve campaign
      Download Email Address To download email address
      Export Campaign Report To export campaign report

  3. To activate the new account

    1. Once a new account is created, a verification email will be sent to the login email
    2. Go to your mail box and click the activation link
    3. Succesful message pop up

    4. If you cannot receive the verification email, please login with your email and passowrd on Spread
    5. Click “Resend” and repeat step 1-3

    Scroll To Index


  4. Go to master account

    To switch to a master accounts (where you act as Basic User, Email Marketer and Administrators)

    1. Click on the pull down menu in Navigation Bar
    2. Choose the account, then click “Go”

    OR:

    1. Click “My Account” in Navigation Bar
    2. Click “Agent Management” to view all accounts you are managing
    3. Click “Login” beside any of them
